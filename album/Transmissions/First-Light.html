First Light

Cascading waves of change

The future

Events we think to be inconsequential,

Can affect the future unintentionally


What if you had the power to affect monumental change.

Would you let fear consume or would you overcome?


We sent a message into the past, a warning

We have that message

Though this story is not inevitable,

And a society does evolve.

We can change our fate,

We can change the future,

We can change the past.
